DRP Designs by David Pugh

My first project was for a friend of my wife, they ran JADE Realty Group, Inc. I started out helping setup their computer system, network and accounting software. Their acconting software was not designed for property management so I created a custom database application for them. Their website was my first venture into web design.

Next came Half Pint Ranch. My neighbor and best friend moved back to his home state of Colorado where he bought a 65-acre ranch east of Denver. He raised miniature Hereford cattle, goats, sheep, chickens and a few pigs. I built his website HalfPintRanch.com to advertise his cattle, goat milk & cheese and fresh eggs.

I currently volunteer for the Orlando Ski and Travel Club as their webmaster. They use Wild Apricot, a membership management software. Wild Apricot host their website and provides a WYSIWYG editor. I use HTML and CSS to improve and add features.
